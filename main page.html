About Us

We often try to deal with problems on our own and in many cases we are successful. However, in some cases we may not have the right skills set to deal with the problems we face in life, work, relationships and situations. These can compound quickly and impact our mental health. This is when counseling is needed. Counseling helps you to speak the truth without any filters and pause. This helps you to find healing and peace within.

  • Personal Counseling
  • Educational Counseling

If you’re feeling confused about your feelings or any decisions, it’s always best to find clarity through speaking with a professional. It helps you to become better person and develop new traits and behavior. In any way, we have Aura to help you. Our counselors are here to help you in any way you need and, more importantly, they care deeply about helping you improve yourself. Thus, a little counseling can help everyone to improve in their everyday life.
